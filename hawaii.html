Hawaii Information

Hawaii has many Nicknames, including: The Aloha State, Paradise of the Pacific and The Islands of Aloha. The capital of Hawaii is Honolulu, which also happens to be their largest city. They have a population of 1,431,603 people and a density of 214 people per square mile. The highest point is Mauna Kea at 13,796 ft. Hawaii's median household income is $64,514 is is ranked 12th highest out of the 50 states
